<p><ul><li>Traditional Packaging</li><li>Advanced Packaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IC packaging services market encompasses two main types: traditional packaging and advanced packaging. Traditional packaging involves established methods such as wire bonding and dual in-line packages (DIPs) that provide essential protection and functionality for integrated circuits. In contrast, advanced packaging utilizes cutting-edge techniques like system-in-package (SiP) and 3D packaging, which offer enhanced performance, miniaturization, and integration of multiple functions. This distinction highlights the evolution in semiconductor packaging, catering to the increasing demands for efficiency and compactness in modern electronics.</p></p>

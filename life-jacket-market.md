<p><strong>The Life Jacket Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inherent life jackets</li><li>Inflatable life jackets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The life jacket market primarily consists of two types: inherent life jackets and inflatable life jackets. Inherent life jackets are made from buoyant materials that provide flotation without the need for inflation, making them ready for immediate use. They are typically bulkier and less portable. In contrast, inflatable life jackets are compact and lightweight, automatically or manually inflating when in water, offering a comfortable fit and reduced bulk when not in use. Each type caters to different user preferences and safety needs.</p></p>

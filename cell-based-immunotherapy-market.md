<p><p>The cell-based immunotherapy market encompasses various types aimed at enhancing the body's immune response against diseases, particularly cancer. Autologous cellular immunotherapy involves the collection and modification of a patient's own cells to target tumors. Chimeric Antigen Receptor (CAR) T-Cell Therapy utilizes genetically engineered T-cells to identify and attack cancer cells specifically. Dendritic cell-based vaccine therapy employs dendritic cells to present antigens that activate the immune system, aiding in targeted treatment and increased immune sensitivity against cancer.</p></p>

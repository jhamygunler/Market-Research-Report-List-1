<p><p>The Centralized Automated Dispensing Cabinets market comprises two main types: stationary and mobile dispensing cabinets. Stationary dispensing cabinets are fixed units, strategically placed within healthcare facilities to efficiently store and dispense medications, enhancing workflow and security. In contrast, mobile dispensing cabinets are portable units that can be easily moved throughout a facility, providing flexibility and quick access to medications at the point of care. Both types aim to improve medication management, reduce errors, and optimize inventory control in healthcare environments.</p></p>

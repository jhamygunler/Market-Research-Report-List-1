<p><p>The warehouse robotics market consists of two main types: mobile robotics and fixed robotics. Mobile robotics involves autonomous machines that navigate dynamically through spaces, delivering goods and optimizing workflows, enhancing flexibility and operational efficiency. In contrast, fixed robotics includes stationary systems like conveyor belts and automated storage systems, designed for repetitive tasks within a set area, ensuring high-speed processing. Both types work synergistically to streamline warehouse operations, improve accuracy, and reduce labor costs, driving growth in the logistics sector.</p></p>

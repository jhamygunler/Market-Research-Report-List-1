<p><strong>The Digital Valve Positioner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Acting Positioner</li><li>Double Acting Positioner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital valve positioner market is categorized into single-acting and double-acting positioners. Single-acting positioners utilize a spring mechanism to return the valve to a default position, making them simpler and cost-effective, suitable for applications with less demanding control. In contrast, double-acting positioners use air pressure on both sides, offering more precise control and faster response times, ideal for high-performance applications requiring accurate valve positioning. Both types enhance automation and efficiency in various industrial processes.</p></p>

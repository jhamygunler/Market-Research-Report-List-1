<p><p>The rail transit traction converter market primarily includes two types: Propulsion Converters and Integrated Compact Converters. Propulsion Converters are dedicated systems that manage the conversion of electrical power for traction motors, ensuring efficient propulsion and acceleration of trains. Integrated Compact Converters, on the other hand, combine multiple functions into a single, space-saving unit, integrating traction power with auxiliary systems. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing energy efficiency, reducing weight, and optimizing overall performance in rail transit systems.</p></p>

<p><p>The solder ribbon market is categorized into four main types based on the primary metal used. Au-based (gold-based) solder ribbons are valued for their excellent corrosion resistance and conductivity, making them ideal for high-end electronics. Ag-based (silver-based) options offer good thermal and electrical properties, commonly used in automotive and industrial applications. In-based (indium-based) solder ribbons provide low melting points and flexibility, suitable for sensitive devices. Other types may include various metal alloys tailored for specific applications across different industries.</p></p>

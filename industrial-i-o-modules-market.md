<p><ul><li>PLC I/O Modules</li><li>DCS I/O Modules</li><li>PC-based I/O Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial I/O modules are essential components in automation systems, categorized into three main types: PLC I/O modules, DCS I/O modules, and PC-based I/O modules. PLC I/O modules facilitate the connection of input/output devices in programmable logic controllers, enabling real-time processing of discrete and analog signals. DCS I/O modules support distributed control systems, optimizing continuous processes across various locations. PC-based I/O modules integrate with computer systems, providing flexibility and advanced processing capabilities for diverse industrial applications, enhancing overall operational efficiency.</p></p>

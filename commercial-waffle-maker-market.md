<p><p>The commercial waffle maker market is segmented into single-grid and double-grid types. Single-grid waffle makers produce one waffle at a time, ideal for smaller establishments or those with limited space. They are often more energy-efficient and easier to clean. In contrast, double-grid waffle makers can prepare two waffles simultaneously, making them suitable for high-demand environments like hotels and cafes. This type offers increased productivity, catering to larger customer volumes while maintaining consistent quality.</p></p>

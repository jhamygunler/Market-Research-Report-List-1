<p><ul><li>Axial Variable Displacement Piston Pump</li><li>Radial Variable Displacement Piston Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The variable displacement piston pump market includes two primary types: axial and radial variable displacement piston pumps. Axial variable displacement pumps feature pistons arranged along the axis of the drive shaft, allowing for efficient flow adjustments and compact designs. In contrast, radial variable displacement pumps have pistons organized radially around the drive shaft, offering higher power density and better performance in various industrial applications. Both types are crucial for providing adaptable hydraulic solutions across diverse sectors, including manufacturing and construction.</p></p>

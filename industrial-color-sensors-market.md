<p><p>The industrial color sensors market is categorized into single-channel and multi-channel types. Single-channel sensors detect one color component, making them cost-effective and suitable for basic applications. In contrast, multi-channel sensors can measure multiple color wavelengths simultaneously, providing more detailed color information and enabling precise quality control in diverse manufacturing processes. Multi-channel sensors are ideal for complex tasks requiring high accuracy, such as food and beverage production, textile inspection, and plastics manufacturing, enhancing product consistency and compliance with standards.</p></p>

<p><p>Recombinant DNA (rDNA) technology encompasses various market types, primarily focusing on expression systems and cloning vectors. Expression systems include prokaryotic and eukaryotic methods, which facilitate the production of proteins or other molecules from inserted genes. Cloning vectors, such as plasmids and viruses, are tools used to transfer genetic material into host cells for replication and expression. Together, these components enable advancements in gene therapy, pharmaceuticals, and biotechnology, driving innovation and growth in the rDNA technology market.</p></p>

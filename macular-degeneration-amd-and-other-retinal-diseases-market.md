<p><p>Macular Degeneration (AMD) and other retinal diseases encompass various eye conditions affecting the retina. AMD is divided into dry (dAMD) and wet (wAMD) types, with dAMD being more common and wAMD causing severe vision loss. Diabetic Macular Edema (DME) and Diabetic Retinopathy (DR) are complications of diabetes that lead to fluid accumulation and retinal damage, respectively. Retinal Vein Occlusion (RVO) involves blocked veins in the retina, affecting blood flow. Together, these conditions significantly impact vision and quality of life.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Safety Needles and Syringes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Safety Needles</li><li>Passive Safety Needles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The safety needles and syringes market is divided into two main types: active safety needles and passive safety needles. Active safety needles require the user to engage a mechanism, such as a sheath or cover, to protect against needle stick injuries after use. In contrast, passive safety needles automatically incorporate safety features that activate without user intervention once the needle is withdrawn, minimizing the risk of accidental pricks. Both types aim to enhance safety in medical settings, reducing infection risks and promoting user confidence.</p></p>

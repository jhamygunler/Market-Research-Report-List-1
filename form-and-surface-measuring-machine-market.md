<p><p>The Form and Surface Measuring Machine market is categorized into two main types: contact and non-contact. Contact type machines utilize physical probes to measure surface profiles and geometrical features, providing high precision for detailed analysis. In contrast, non-contact type machines leverage optical or laser technology to capture surface measurements without physical interaction, offering faster data acquisition and reduced wear on samples. Both types are pivotal in industries such as manufacturing, aerospace, and automotive for quality control and product development.</p></p>

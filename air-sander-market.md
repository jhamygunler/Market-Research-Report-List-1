<p><p>The air sander market includes various types of sanders, primarily orbital sanders and belt sanders. Orbital sanders utilize a circular sanding pad that moves in a random orbit, offering a smooth finish for fine sanding and polishing. Belt sanders, on the other hand, feature a continuous loop of sandpaper and are ideal for removing material quickly on larger surfaces. Each type serves distinct purposes in woodworking and metalworking, catering to both professional and DIY users' needs for efficiency and precision.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Flat Flange Nut</li><li>Serrated Flange Nut</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flange nut market includes two primary types: flat flange nuts and serrated flange nuts. Flat flange nuts feature a smooth, wide bearing surface that helps distribute load evenly and prevent damage to the material being fastened, making them ideal for applications requiring stability. Serrated flange nuts, on the other hand, have angled serrations on the flange that provide enhanced grip and resistance to loosening under vibration, making them suitable for dynamic environments. Both types are essential in various industrial and automotive applications.</p></p>

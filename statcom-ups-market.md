<p><ul><li>Gate Turn-Off Thyristors(GTO) STATCOM</li><li>Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistors(IGBT) STATCOM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The STATCOM UPS market is segmented into two primary types based on the switching technology used: Gate Turn-Off Thyristors (GTO) STATCOM and Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistors (IGBT) STATCOM. GTO STATCOMs are known for their high power handling capabilities and robust performance in voltage regulation applications, while IGBT STATCOMs offer faster switching speeds, higher efficiency, and compact designs, making them suitable for dynamic reactive power compensation in modern power systems. Each type serves distinct applications in enhancing grid stability and reliability.</p></p>

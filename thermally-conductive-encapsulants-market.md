<p><p>The thermally conductive encapsulants market is primarily segmented into two types: silicon type and epoxy type. Silicon-based encapsulants offer excellent thermal conduction, flexibility, and resistance to aging, making them suitable for high-performance applications in electronics and automotive sectors. Conversely, epoxy-based encapsulants provide superior adhesion, mechanical strength, and stability under thermal stress, ideal for applications requiring rigorous durability. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing heat dissipation in electronic components, optimizing performance, and extending product lifespan.</p></p>

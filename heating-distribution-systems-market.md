<p><p>Heating distribution systems encompass various market types designed to provide warmth in residential and commercial settings. Radiators use hot water or steam to distribute heat, while furnaces generate heated air circulated through ducts. Boilers produce hot water for heating and can work alongside radiators. Heat pumps transfer heat from the ground or air, offering energy-efficient solutions. Active solar heating harnesses solar energy for warmth, while electric heating uses electric resistance for direct heat. Additional systems may include underfloor heating and hybrid models.</p></p>

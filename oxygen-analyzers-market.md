<p><p>The oxygen analyzers market is primarily categorized into trace oxygen analyzers and online oxygen analyzers. Trace oxygen analyzers detect very low levels of oxygen, typically in applications where maintaining specific oxygen concentrations is critical, such as in pharmaceuticals and food packaging. Online oxygen analyzers, on the other hand, continuously monitor oxygen levels in real-time within industrial processes, ensuring optimal conditions for production and safety in sectors like oil and gas, water treatment, and chemical manufacturing, enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>

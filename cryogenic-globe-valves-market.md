<p><p>The cryogenic globe valves market is categorized into three main types: butt welding, flange, and thread types. Butt welding valves provide seamless connections and are preferred for high-pressure applications, ensuring minimal leakage. Flange-type valves feature bolted connections, allowing for easier installation and maintenance, making them suitable for large piping systems. Thread-type valves are compact and ideal for smaller systems, offering easy assembly and disassembly. Each type is designed to maintain performance in extremely low-temperature conditions, crucial for cryogenic applications.</p></p>

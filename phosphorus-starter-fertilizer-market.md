<p><ul><li>Ortho-phosphate</li><li>Poly-phosphate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The phosphorus starter fertilizer market is primarily divided into two types: ortho-phosphate and poly-phosphate. Ortho-phosphate, the most common form, is immediately available for plant uptake, making it ideal for enhancing early growth. In contrast, poly-phosphate is a more complex compound that offers a sustained release of phosphorus, promoting long-term nutrient availability. Both types help optimize phosphorus nutrition in crops, supporting healthy development and improving yields, but they cater to different agricultural needs based on soil chemistry and crop requirements.</p></p>

<p><p>A Dual-chamber Pacemaker is a medical device designed to regulate heart rhythm by stimulating both the atrium and ventricle in patients with heart rhythm disorders, such as bradycardia. It consists of two leads—one placed in the right atrium and the other in the right ventricle—allowing the pacemaker to coordinate heartbeats more effectively than single-chamber devices. This capability is especially beneficial for patients with atrioventricular (AV) block or other complex arrhythmias.</p><p>The Dual-chamber Pacemaker Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.2% during the forecast period, driven by several factors.

<p><p>The polywoven bags market comprises two primary types: liner and non-liner polywoven bags. Liner polywoven bags feature an internal lining that provides enhanced moisture resistance and protection for sensitive contents, making them ideal for agricultural and food packaging applications. Non-liner polywoven bags, on the other hand, do not have an internal layer and are generally used for bulk items like grains, building materials, and chemicals, offering durability and strength for heavy loads. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>

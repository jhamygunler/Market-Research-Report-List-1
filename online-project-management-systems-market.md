<p><p>Online project management systems can be categorized into two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise systems are installed locally on a company's servers, providing full control over data and security but requiring significant maintenance and hardware investment. In contrast, cloud-based systems operate on remote servers accessed via the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and ease of collaboration. They typically involve subscription models, enabling organizations to reduce upfront costs while benefiting from automatic updates and lower maintenance responsibilities.</p></p>

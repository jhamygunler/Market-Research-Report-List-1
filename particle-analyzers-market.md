Laser particle analyzers utilize laser diffraction to measure particle size and distribution, providing rapid and precise results suitable for diverse applications. In contrast, image particle analyzers employ imaging techniques to capture and analyze particle shapes and distributions, offering detailed morphological insights. Both types serve critical roles in industries such as pharmaceuticals, materials science, and environmental monitoring, facilitating quality control and research efforts.</p></p>

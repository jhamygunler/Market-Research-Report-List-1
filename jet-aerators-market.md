<p><strong>The Jet Aerators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Compressed Air Type</li><li>Self-Aspirating Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The jet aerators market can be categorized primarily into two types: compressed air type and self-aspirating type. Compressed air jet aerators utilize external air sources to inject air into the water, enhancing oxygen transfer efficiency in wastewater treatment. In contrast, self-aspirating jet aerators draw in ambient air through their design, relying on fluid dynamics to mix air with water without needing an external air supply. Both types serve crucial roles in improving water quality in various applications, including industrial and municipal settings.</p></p>

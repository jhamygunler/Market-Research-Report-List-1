<p><p>Hand flares are essential signaling tools used in emergencies, categorized mainly into two market types: distress flares and smoke signal flares. Distress flares emit bright colors and high-intensity light to attract attention in life-threatening situations, typically used in maritime and aerial emergencies. Smoke signal flares, on the other hand, produce dense smoke to mark locations or signal distress over longer durations, often employed in search and rescue operations. Both types are vital for ensuring safety and effective communication during critical situations.</p></p>

<p><p>The commercial elevator market encompasses three primary types: elevators, escalators, and moving walkways. Elevators are vertical transportation systems designed to move people and goods between floors in buildings, enhancing accessibility. Escalators provide continuous, inclined moving stairs for convenient access in high-traffic areas like malls and airports. Moving walkways, or travelators, facilitate horizontal transportation over longer distances, improving pedestrian flow in places such as airports and large venues. Together, these systems enhance mobility and efficiency in commercial spaces.</p></p>

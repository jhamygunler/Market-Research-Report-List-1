<p><p>The fire hydrant market encompasses various types based on their operational mechanisms. Compression type hydrants use a screw mechanism for water flow control, allowing for high durability and reduced leakage. Toggle type hydrants employ a lever system for easy operation, facilitating quick access during emergencies. Slide gate type hydrants involve a sliding mechanism, ideal for low-pressure systems. High-pressure hydrants are designed to withstand elevated water pressure, ensuring effective firefighting capabilities in urban environments. Each type serves unique applications in fire safety.</p></p>

<p><p>The Circuit Breakers and Fuses market encompasses two primary types of electrical protection devices. Circuit breakers are mechanical switches that automatically interrupt electrical flow during fault conditions, ensuring safety and preventing system damage. Fuses, meanwhile, are sacrificial devices that melt to break the circuit when excessive current flows, offering a simpler, cost-effective solution. Both play crucial roles in safeguarding electrical systems across various industries, including residential, commercial, and industrial settings, by preventing overloads and short circuits.</p></p>

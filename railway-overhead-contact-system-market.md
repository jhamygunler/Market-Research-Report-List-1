<p><strong>The Railway Overhead Contact System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Simple Catenary</li><li>Stitched Catenary</li><li>Compound Catenary</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Railway Overhead Contact System market includes three main types: Simple Catenary, Stitched Catenary, and Compound Catenary. </p><p>Simple Catenary features a basic design with a single wire for electric supply, suitable for low-speed railways. Stitched Catenary incorporates a more complex arrangement for enhanced stability and is used in medium-speed applications. Compound Catenary combines elements of both previous types, providing flexibility in high-speed rail environments while maintaining optimal tension and reliability for electric train operations.</p></p>

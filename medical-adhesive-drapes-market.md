<p><p>The medical adhesive drapes market is categorized into two primary types: negative pressure wound therapy drapes and surgical drapes. Negative pressure wound therapy drapes facilitate the treatment of chronic wounds by creating a sealed environment that enhances healing while minimizing infection. Surgical drapes, on the other hand, are used during surgical procedures to maintain a sterile field, protect patients from contaminants, and ensure optimal visibility for surgeons. Both types are essential for improving patient outcomes and ensuring procedural efficiency.</p></p>

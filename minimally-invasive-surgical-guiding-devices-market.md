<p><p>The minimally invasive surgical guiding devices market primarily includes guiding catheters and guidewires. Guiding catheters are flexible tubes that assist in navigating blood vessels or other pathways to deliver instruments or fluids precisely. Guidewires are thin, flexible wires used to navigate through complex anatomical structures, providing support and stability during procedures. Together, these devices enhance the accuracy and safety of minimally invasive surgeries, allowing for smaller incisions, reduced recovery times, and improved patient outcomes.</p></p>

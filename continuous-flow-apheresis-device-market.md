<p><p>The Continuous Flow Apheresis Device market consists of various types tailored for specific therapeutic applications. Immunoadsorption columns target specific antibodies or proteins for removal, enhancing autoimmune treatment. Plasma perfusion columns are designed for continuous plasma exchange, aiding in disease management. Hemoperfusion columns purify blood by removing toxins, making them essential for treating certain conditions. Each type plays a crucial role in advanced therapeutic procedures.</p></p>

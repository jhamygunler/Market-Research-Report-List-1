<p><p>The Remote Sensing Infrared LED market comprises three main types: Near-Infrared (NIR) LEDs, Mid-Infrared (MIR) LEDs, and Far-Infrared (FIR) LEDs. NIR LEDs are primarily used for applications like optical communication and sensing due to their short wavelengths. MIR LEDs operate in a range suitable for materials characterization and biomedical applications. FIR LEDs emit longer wavelengths, making them ideal for thermal imaging and night vision technologies. Each type serves distinct functions, driven by their wavelength-specific properties.</p></p>

<p><p>The magnetometer sensor market is primarily categorized into Hall Effect sensors and MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) sensors. Hall Effect sensors operate by detecting magnetic fields through voltage generation in conductive materials, providing robust performance in various applications, such as automotive and industrial settings. Conversely, MEMS sensors utilize tiny mechanical structures to sense magnetic fields, offering high sensitivity and compact design ideal for consumer electronics and portable devices. Both types are essential for navigation, location tracking, and magnetic field measurement.</p></p>

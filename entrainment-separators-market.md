<p><ul><li>Wire-mesh Entrainment Separators</li><li>Baffle-type Entrainment Separators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Entrainment separators are devices used to remove liquid droplets from gas streams in industrial applications. The wire-mesh type utilizes a network of fine wires to capture and coalesce droplets, effectively enhancing separation efficiency. In contrast, baffle-type separators employ physical barriers or baffles to change gas flow direction, facilitating droplet disengagement and collection. Both types serve essential roles in processes like natural gas processing and petrochemical operations, ensuring cleaner gas outputs and improving overall system performance.</p></p>

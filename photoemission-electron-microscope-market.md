<p><p>The Photoemission Electron Microscope (PEEM) market comprises two main types: Ultraviolet Photoemission Electron Microscopes (UPEEM) and X-ray Photoemission Electron Microscopes (XPEEM). UPEEM utilizes ultraviolet light to excite electrons, allowing for enhanced imaging of surface electronic structures and properties with high spatial resolution. In contrast, XPEEM employs X-rays, providing deeper insight into chemical states and elemental distributions within materials. Both types facilitate advanced research in nanotechnology, materials science, and semiconductor analysis, catering to diverse applications in academia and industry.</p></p>

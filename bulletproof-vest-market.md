<p><ul><li>Soft Bulletproof Vest</li><li>Hard Bulletproof Vest</li><li>Composite Bulletproof Vest</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bulletproof vest market consists of three primary types: soft, hard, and composite bulletproof vests. Soft bulletproof vests are made from flexible materials, offering comfort and concealability, primarily designed for low-level ballistic threats. Hard bulletproof vests incorporate rigid plates, providing higher protection against more severe impacts but with less mobility. Composite bulletproof vests combine both soft and hard materials, aiming to balance comfort, mobility, and enhanced protection, catering to a diverse range of tactical and personal safety needs.</p></p>

<p><p>The polypropylene honeycomb market is primarily categorized into monolayer and multilayer types. Monolayer polypropylene honeycomb consists of a single layer of cells, offering lightweight and rigid properties suitable for various applications, such as packaging and construction. Conversely, multilayer polypropylene honeycomb features multiple layers, enhancing strength, insulation, and impact resistance, making it ideal for demanding applications like automotive and aerospace. Both types provide unique advantages tailored to specific product requirements, driving their use across diverse industries.</p></p>
